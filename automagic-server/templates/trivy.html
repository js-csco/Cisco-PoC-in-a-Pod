Trivy — Vulnerability Scanner

Scans every container image in your k3s cluster for known CVEs, misconfigurations, and exposed secrets.

Tetragon watches what your containers do. Trivy checks what they're vulnerable to.

Runtime detection catches threats in the act — a shell spawned in a production pod, a process reading /etc/shadow, a connection to a suspicious IP. But by the time Tetragon fires, the attacker is already inside.

Trivy works the other side of the problem. It scans every container image running in your k3s cluster for known CVEs — the open doors an attacker might walk through. A critical vulnerability in your base image. An outdated library with a published exploit. A container running as root.

Together, they close the loop: Trivy tells you where you're exposed. Tetragon tells you when someone tries to exploit it.
