WHAT IS CALDERA?

Caldera is MITRE's open-source adversary emulation platform. It runs real attack techniques — not simulations — using a lightweight agent called sandcat that deploys into a victim pod and executes commands on behalf of the C2 server.

In this demo, Caldera plays the attacker: it runs structured MITRE ATT&CK techniques (recon, credential theft, persistence) inside the victim pod while Tetragon watches every syscall at the kernel level. The goal is to show that even a fully autonomous attacker cannot operate undetected inside this cluster.
